About

I'm an Automation & Control engineer working across data and AI. I like problems where a good model, some SQL, and a bit of automation quietly remove weeks of manual work.

At Reply I work as a Data & AI Consultant: BI platforms and analytical layers (Looker, BigQuery, Python, Databricks) for enterprise clients, plus internal AI initiatives, an LLM orchestrator that migrates BI dashboards via MCP and a RAG app for change-impact analysis.
